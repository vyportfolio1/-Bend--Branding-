This project establishes a cohesive, graffiti-inspired brand identity for a cutting-edge clothing line, focusing on urban and war-inspired themes

Brand Voice and Messaging

The brand voice of "Bend" is bold, rebellious, and unapologetically urban. It reflects the raw energy of street culture, using powerful, direct language that resonates with the modern, edgy consumer. Our messaging speaks to those who embrace individuality and defy conventional norms, aligning with the gritty aesthetics of graffiti and urban warfare

Urban Warriors

The "Urban Warriors" collection embodies resilience and strength, featuring war-inspired designs with a modern twist. Each piece channels the fierce spirit of street culture and urban conflict

Visual Identity

"Bend's" visual identity is grounded in graffiti art, blending bold, street-style elements with contemporary design. The use of dynamic textures, urban motifs, and gritty, hand-drawn elements creates a visually arresting brand presence. The logo, product images, and packaging all reflect this cohesive aesthetic, ensuring a seamless brand experience. The edgy visual elements are balanced with clean layouts and organized content, making the brand both impactful and approachable

71D4E6

Colors

The primary color, #000000, represents the bold, edgy essence of the brand, echoing the darkness of urban environments. The accent color, #71D4E6, adds a pop of vibrancy, symbolizing creativity and innovation amidst the gritty backdrop. This contrast highlights key elements, ensuring the brand remains visually striking and memorable while maintaining a cohesive, urban-inspired palette

Font family

For titles, "Modak" font is used to inject personality and flair into the brand's bold statements, while "NanumGothic" serves as the primary font for main content, offering a clean, modern readability. The custom graffiti-style font used in the logo reinforces the brands unique identity, bridging street art with a professional edge

Logo Integration

The custom-designed graffiti-style logo is integral to the "Bend" brand, embodying the rebellious spirit and urban roots of the collection. This logo is seamlessly integrated across all touchpoints, from clothing tags to digital platforms, ensuring a consistent brand identity. Its bold design captures attention and reinforces the brand's commitment to edgy, street-inspired fashion

Brand Story

Born from the streets, this brand celebrates the resilience and defiant spirit of urban culture. Inspired by the raw energy of graffiti and the strength of modern-day warriors, it stands for those who break boundaries and reject conformity. Each piece is more than just clothingit's a statement, a symbol of rebellion, and a tribute to street culture

Social Media Presence

"Bend" maintains an active social media presence, reflecting the brands urban and rebellious vibe. Content includes behind-the-scenes looks, user-generated content, and graffiti-inspired visuals that engage the audience. Consistent branding across platforms strengthens recognition, building a community of like-minded individuals who resonate with the brands identity
